Currently Available in Test Chamber

  • Deluxe Mr. Kalw "Skill" Crane Game [default]
    • You can pull items 3 times per day.
    • There is a very low chance to obtain a familiar. (gift item, untradeable)
  • A Relaxing Hot Tub [default]
    • Fully restores your HP. (same effect as Scroll of Drastic Healing)
    • Can be used up to 5 times per day.
  • A Fax Machine [ June, 2025 - Lazulite ]
    • Once per day, you can send one monster copy or receive a copy sent by another clannie.
    • By using Faxbot, you can receive a copy of the monster you want.
  • Clan Photo Booth [ Dec. 16. 2025 - Riebeck ]
    • Currently available in standard.
    • You can receive buffs up to 3 times per day.
    • You can also borrow up to 3 pieces of equipment per day. (duplicates allowed, meltable)
    • You can take group photos with other members' characters. (just for fun)
  • Underground Fireworks Shop [ Jan. 12. 2026 - Riebeck ]
    • You can buy one type of hat and one piece of equipment once per day. (meltable)
    • Blue Rocket restore 1,000 MP, and Red Rocket increase the EXP from your next food by 4x after use.
    • If you don't have any Yellow Ray items, Yellow Rocket is also a good option.
  • Fortune Teller [Jan, 23. 2026 - Riebeck]
    • You can consult with a NPC to receive a buff. (100 turns, once per day)
    • You can consult with other clannies. (3 times per day)
      • Depending on compatibility, you may receive items or equipment at random.
      • You don't need to have a Clan VIP Lounge key to be consulted, but you cannot respond.
