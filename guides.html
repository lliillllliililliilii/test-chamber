Customize Your Menu

In the top-right corner, you will see several small menu icons.
From there, click EDIT to customize your menu or add shortcuts.

Other icons

  • Two circles and one dot: the two moons and a moon satellite.
  • Question : The help page
  • Bug : The bug report page
  • ZZZ : logout

How to Chat

On the main map, you'll see The Big Mountains

If you go there, you'll find The Temple of Literacy. Pass the test there.

Once you've passed the temple's test, you'll see "Enter the Chat" at the very top of the right-hand frame.
Click that to enter the chat.

Useful Chat Commands

  • Chat Commands - Type /? or /help in the chat
  • Join a channel - /c [channel name] or /channel [channel name]
  • Listen to a channel :
    • /l [channel name] or /listen [channel name]
  • Chat channels
    • /c newbie : a channel for newbies
    • /c clan : your clan's channel

DO NOT use The Flea Market!

There are scammers mixed in there.
Prices cannot be checked properly, and because it's very easy to access, scammers often sell cheap items highter than normal price.

So we strongly recommend using The Mall instead.

How to Make Meat

1. Basic Meat Farming

Check out the wiki.

  • Basic Farming

2. 11-leaf Clovers

This is a way to earn a large amount of meat quickly.(25k-30k each) Just exchange 3 per day and try selling them.

If you go to The Big Mountains, you will find The Hermitage.

Here, you can exchange up to three 11-leaf clovers per day.

The useless trinkets required for the exchange can be obtained randomly by using Chewing gum on a string from The General Store. /go general
About 15-20 gums should be enough.

Sell the 11-leaf clovers at The Mall.

If your level is too low or you do not have enough Meat to open a shop, list them on The Flea Market for around 25k-30k Meat.

Q. Wait, is it ok to sell them for such a high price?

A. Yes. This item has very high demand, which is why the price is high.

3. The Mall of Loathing

This is a place where you can sell your items to other players.
To open your own store, you will need some meat.

Type /go mall or /go search the mall in chat. You can also add a shortcut for it.

Or, follow the image below.

If your level is too low, level up first before using the mall.
