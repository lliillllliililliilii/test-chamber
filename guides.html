How to chat

On the main map, you'll see The Big Mountains

If you go there, you'll find The Temple of Literacy. Pass the test there.

Once you've passed the temple's test, you'll see "Enter the Chat" at the very top of the right-hand frame.
Click that to enter the chat.

Clan guide

What is Clan?

In short, it’s like a guild or a group.

When you join a clan, you can use items stored in the clan stash, and receive buffs and extra adventures from the rumpus room. You can also talk with other clannies in a dedicated chat channel and ask for help. Try typing /c clan or /listen clan.

If you are an experienced player, you can also attempt the dungeons located in the clan basement.

Below is the clan hall you can visit after joining a clan. Type /go clan in chat, or add it to your top menu.

You don't have to stay in only one clan. By using the whitelist feature, you can freely move between multiple clans.
Ask a clan staff member or the clan leader about this.
