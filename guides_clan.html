Clan guide

What is Clan?

In short, it's like a guild or a group.

When you join a clan, you can use items stored in the clan stash, and receive buffs and extra adventures from the rumpus room. You can also talk with other clannies in a dedicated chat channel and ask for help. Try typing /c clan or /listen clan.

If you are an experienced player, you can also attempt the dungeons located in the clan basement.

Below is the clan hall you can visit after joining a clan. Type /go clan in chat, or add it to your top menu.

Joining a clan will give you access to these :

  • Clan Stash
    • plenty of items shared within the clan
  • Rumpus Room
    • provides extra buffs, advs, a bit of meat (varies)
  • VIP Lounge
    • provides exclusive items and buffs
    • need a clan VIP lounge key
  • Clan Basement
    • clan dungeons

You don't have to stay in just one clan. By using the whitelist feature, you can move between multiple clans.
Ask a staff member or the leader in your clan about this.

For more detailed information, check out the wiki.

Q. How do I join a clan?

1. Finding a clan

Method A: Clan Recruiter

Go to The Clan District in Seaside Town, where you can see a search bar and a list of the top 10 clans

Click the name of the clan you want to join.
Also, you can search for a clan that your friend is in.

Method B : Player Profile

You might want to join the clan of a player who helped you.
Or you may have received a clan invitation through chat or messages.

In this case, click the player's nickname.

2. Applying to a clan

No matter which method you use, you will see the clan information page.
Scroll down below the member list and you will find a checkbox and a button like these.

Click the checkbox first, then click the button, and wait for the clan's staff or leader to accept it.
